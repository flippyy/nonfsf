Sarah Adelaida McIntire

by Matt Lee Contributions — Published on Sep 08, 2009 03:57 PM
Sarah was an intern at the FSF during the summer of 2009

Adelaida is an intern at the FSF interested in the ethical concerns behind the free software movement. She started using free software less than a year ago and is proof that you do not need to know how a computer works to understand the importance of free software. She will be working as part of the FSF campaigns team.

Adelaida is from Ann Arbor, Michigan and currently attends Hampshire College. She plans to study philosophy, feminism and gender.
