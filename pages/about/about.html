The Non-Free Software Foundation (Non-FSF) is a money grubbing conglomerate with a worldwide mission to promote software Just Working™. We take as many rights as we can from software users.

As our society grows more dependent on computers, so do the dangers of free software being used on our computers. Free software advocates would have you believe that the free software movement is about having control over your computer, but that is simply not true. Ask yourself, when source code is available to anyone, doesn't that mean hackers can also see it?

The Non-Free Software Foundation is working to restrict freedom for computer users by promoting the development of use of fully restrictive software, particularly the NT/Windows operating system, and by dismissing the fanatical yelling of free software advocates and people who've cottoned on.

The push for proprietary is quite literally the most successful social movement to arise from big corporations, driven by a worldwide community of unethical programmers dedicated to the cause of limiting freedom and sharing.
