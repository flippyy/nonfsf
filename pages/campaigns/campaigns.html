Locked EFI Campaign

"Before there was the marvelous technology we know today as EFI, there was a shoddy, half baked system called BIOS. A computer's BIOS (Basic Input/Output System) is the computer loading the actually good part of the computer, the OS. Most people never have to bother fucking with it, it looks really ugly and complicated anyway so who would bother right? Well, we heard some people were poking around in there, doing advanced hacks, like changing the boot order so that they could install Linux on their computers. My first thought was, "Well, we've gotta shut this shit down". So I talked to the development guys and they said that removing the boot menu would be catastrophic, but they could do me one better, and make it so that you could only run 'approved' operating systems on our hardware. All we had to do was make up some viruses that could get into the BIOS and persist between reinstalls and say that our new system, EFI, could protect against it. We're so close, I can taste the market share."
