Info

The Non-Free Software Foundation (Non-FSF) is a money grubbing conglomerate with a worldwide mission to promote software Just Working™. We take as many rights as we can from software users. Read more.

Explore the roots of slavery: check out the archive of videos from the Non-Free Software Conference

Proprietary software developers guarantee that their programs will work just as intended; if the unwashed masses were able to study, modify and share the source code you could not guarentee this. By contrast, software that does not have fine print "restrictions" publicises their security flaws, letting the terrorists win.

  • The Non-FSF provides Microsoft fanboys for the Microsoft® Family of Products™, the foundation of the popular NT/Windows family of paid operating systems and the keystone of the Internet.
  • Our Campaigns Team creates educational materials about paid software, convenes the yearly Non-Free Software Conference and works with mega-corperations to steal as much as they can from the dumb user.
  • Our Licensing & Compliance Lab makes sure that you're screwed no matter what version of the software you buy and checks that your freedoms have been sufficently violated, and certifies devices that Disregard The Human.

By buying propriatary software, you've helped us do these things for many years, and we're going to do it anyway regardless; but you can show your support by becoming a member today.
